About Me

Hi, this is GiGi!

I'm an aspiring Data Scientist with previous experience in Education and E-commerce industries. I am passionate in wrangling with complex data and applying machine learning to tell stories and solve meaningful business problems.

I'm graduating soon from my Bachelor's of Science in Statistics at the University of California - Los Angeles (UCLA), where I have developed a strong programming , statistics and analytics skill set that can tackle business problems and generate meaningful insights for social impact.

As a Data Analyst intern at Elsa Speak for approximately 2 years, my primary role is to work cross-functionally with product and marketing teams to connect mobile usage with business outcomes, manage A/B testing, answer ad hoc questions for product decisions, and conduct data analysis on user behavior to promote engagement and retention. .
